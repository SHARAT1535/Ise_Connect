About ISE

Information Science & Engineering

The Department of Information Science and Engineering, established in the year 2000 with an initial intake of 30 students, offers an undergraduate course (B.E. in Information Science and Engineering). Currently, the intake is enhanced to 60 students.

The department is recognized as a research centre by Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum, and has been accredited twice by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

  • Year of Establishment: 2000
  • Head of Department: Dr. Jagadeesh Pujari
  • NBA Accreditation Status: Accredited for Academic Years 2020-2021, 2021-2022, 2022-2023, and further accredited for 2023-24, 2024-25, and 2025-26
  • Current Intake: 60 students

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop competent Information Technology Engineers having complete knowledge and skills in contemporary Information Technology and practices.

Mission

  • To have contextually relevant Curricular.
  • To promote effective Teaching Learning Practices supported by Modern Educational Tools and Techniques.
  • To enhance Research Culture.
  • To involve Industrial Expertise for connecting classroom content to real life situations.
  • To inculcate Ethics and impart soft-skills leading to overall Personality Development.
