Placements @ SDMCET

At SDMCET, we believe that placement performance is one of the vital indicators of overall success of a professional institution. The TPO recognizes the fact that not all students are interested in placement and may have other career plans. But for those who are deserving and willing to walk the preparation path, we have a comprehensive plan that initiates the student towards a successful placement process as early as in the 4th semester, and supports them throughout onboarding.

Solid Placement Record

Internships are increasingly being offered to selected students. In collaboration with CIII and departments, students undergo training in various modules relevant to training and placement. These include aptitude and communication skills, which, combined with technical knowledge, make them placement-ready by the end of the 6th semester.

Every year, over 100 companies visit our campus for recruitment, including national and international brands across all engineering branches. On average, more than 80% of students are placed annually.
